Hello! My name is Souhail, and I enjoy learning and building new things. My interest in development started back in 2014 in my first year of High School — and later turned out to a hobby I spent most my time doing.

In the following years I've had the privilege of working with a PhD candidate on implementing his thesis, two governmental institutions to develop a prototype digitizing their services, as well as a wide range of amazing and supportive professors and instructors.
